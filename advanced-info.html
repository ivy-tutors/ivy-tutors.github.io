Advanced Course Details

These courses are tailored towards older students, typically high schoolers (although advanced middle schoolers are welcome). We offer all courses students may be interested in, from conventional courses like Algebra, Computer Science, Biology, and English to less common ones like Urban Planning, Transportation Engineering, and Food Science.

Courses may be taken either as one-on-one courses or as small group courses (up to five students). Personalized one-on-one courses are slightly more expensive while small group courses have cheaper hourly rates per student. If your student would like a small group, we encourage them to register for courses with their friends; if this is not possible, we will try to match them with other students interested in the class. Keep in mind that if you student wishes to take a less common course like Urban Planning, they will likely have to take it as a one-on-one course unless they have friends that want to take it with them.

Our standard costs are as follows. Unlike many of our competitors, this cost not only encompasses tutoring time, but also resources for the course. Furthermore, please consult our referrals program for additional discounts.

  1. One-on-one: $90/hr
  2. Two-person: $80/hr
  3. Three-person: $70/hr
  4. Four-person: $60/hr
  5. Five-person: $50/hr
With the above in mind, we do not want cost to be a barrier to our program. If cost is an issue, please reach out through our contact form and we will attempt to find a solution.
