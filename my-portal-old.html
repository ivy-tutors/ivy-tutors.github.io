Our pricing is consistently shifting according to tutor availability and relative difficulty of a course. In order to follow this flexibility, we utilize a bidding system for requesting courses.

What is bidding?

Bidding is simply the how much a student is willing to pay for a course. For example, suppose Student A talks to their friend Student B and they want to take a course together. Once Student A and Student B agree on a price, Student A can request a 2 person course at a bid of $70 per hour per student. This means that Student A and Student B are willing to pay $70 an hour each for the course.

What are usual bids for your courses?

Bidding allows for times of greater tutor availability (e.g. during off seasons) to have relatively lower prices. That being said, most successful bids for standard courses are in the following ranges (per student per hour), with more specialized courses (e.g. Olympiad training, niche languages, etc) being slightly more expensive.

  • One-on-one: $90-$120 USD
  • Two Students: $70-$90 USD
  • Three Students: $50-$70 USD
  • Four Students: $40-$50 USD
  • Five or more Students: $30-$40 USD

Once you decided on a course you'd like to take, here's the timeline for requesting a course.

Request the course

Navigate to either "Request a Course" if you are looking for a standard classroom course like Chemistry, US History, or SAT or "Request a Specialized Course" if you would like a course custom made for the studen in subjects like Latin or Olympiad training.

Wait for Processing

If there's a match between your request and a mentor, an email will be sent to the address associated with your account connecting you with your tutor to schedule your first session! After payment is processed, your classes start in earnest. Matching will take at most one week. If your request takes more than one week to fill, it is unlikely that there's a match with a tutor at the current moment.

What to do if there's no match?

Don't fret! It's possible that all tutors are currently just busy at the moment. Feel free to resubmit another request through the form, adjusting any parameters like the student size or the bid as necessary, or contact us at ivytutors2021@gmail.com!

Looking for a place to start? Visit our chatbot if you have any quick questions.

What should I do if I know a course I want to take?

If you know the course you'd like to take, you can either request a normal course or a specialized course, depending on how rare the subject is. Here's a good rule of thumb: if a course is usually offered in a high school setting, it's a normal course. Otherwise, it's a specialized one.

Will there ever be sales or times of lower bid prices?

Yes! We will be intermittently be handing out discounts, which will appear as a banner on this page when they occur. Be sure to check in often!

What if I just want to try out your courses?

If you'd like to try us out, please feel free to check out our masterclasses! We ensure that they are relatively inexpensive, but still offer a good peek into the way we conduct our lessons.

What if I have any questions or concerns?

Please feel free to reach out to us! Feel free to contact us at ivytutors2021@gmail.com with any questions or concerns!
