College Counseling Program Details

The college counseling program is aimed towards all high schoolers, not just rising seniors, as they navigate the ins and outs of academic, extracurricular, and professional life. Our program is specially-tailored to your student; if your student needs community service on their resume, our tutors will recommend one of the most impactful forms tailored to their application, helping them through the process. Thus, our program not only encompasses the typical application, but also preparing for the application, even years ahead of time.

Courses may be taken either as one-on-one courses or as small group courses (up to five students). Personalized one-on-one courses are slightly more expensive while small group courses have cheaper rates per student. If your student would like a small group, we encourage them to register for courses with their friends; if this is not possible, we will try to match them with other students interested in the class.

With the above in mind, we do not want cost to be a barrier to our program. If cost is an issue, please reach out through our contact form and we will attempt to find a solution.
