Our pricing is consistently shifting according to tutor availability and relative difficulty of a course.

How much does a course cost?

Our typical course prices are listed in our course directory where we list course descriptions, example topics in sessions, and standard 1-on-1 pricing. These prices are not set in stone, however! If you know a friend or two that would like to take the course with you or if have people to refer to our organization, the actual price for a course can decrease quite significantly. Once you know of a course you're interested in, go ahead and schedule a free consultation with us!

What are usual standard prices for your courses?

Although prices are subject to tutor availability and the time of year, most of our hourly rate for courses typically follow the following values. Students that work with us for extended periods of time or families who act as referrals will typically get lower rates than listed here.

  • One-on-one: $90-$120 USD/hr each student
  • Two Students: $70-$90 USD/hr each student
  • Three Students: $50-$70 USD/hr each student
  • Four Students: $40-$50 USD/hr each student
  • Five or more Students: $35-$40 USD/hr each student

Once you decided on a course you'd like to take, here's the timeline for requesting a course.

Read the Course Description

Look for the course (or some related subject) in our course directory to get a general sense of the topic. You'll see the general description of the course, an example topic, and the suggested 1-on-1 pricing.

Schedule a Consultation

Schedule a free consultation with us! You'll speak to an experienced mentor and organizer for our group, who will help you customize the course you'd like to take and work through logistical plans. If you'd like to act as a referer or schedule a group course, let us know! The hourly price per student can be dramatically decreased.

Take the course!

After the consultation, you'll be able to start your course within a week at the latest. You will be connected with an organizer for the course as well as the tutor themself!
