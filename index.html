The Ivy Tutors Academy

  • History

The Ivy Tutors Academy started as a humble student group out of Harvard University students dedicated to spreading high-level educational opportunities worldwide.

Our first program

Our first courses launched in March 2022, offering our mentorship services to friends and family in the nearby community. Courses primarily centered around STEM subjects like mathematics or physics. Ever since then, our students hail from all around the United States, China, Japan, Philippines, Germany, India, and more!

Our expansion

After our courses, many of our students have reported being more passionate about their learning, earning higher grades, or (in the case of our college mentorship program) even earning admission into universities like Harvard itself. Experienced in launching worldwide training programs both over Zoom and in-person, our organization now features dozens of specialized instructors from across the Ivy Leagues and T10 colleges in subjects ranging from biology to literature to mathematics and computer science.

Our future

We hope to build out a larger and larger set of opportunities for our students, ranging from affordable masterclasses to in-person courses around the world! Be sure to stay tuned!

Learn more about the numbers that built our organization.

Number of tutors

We currently have about two dozen tutors qualified in subjects from Multivariate Calculus to Film to Poetry from the Ivy Leagues and other T10 schools. Our mentors are published scientific researchers, book authors, Olympiad medallists, big tech interns, and entrepreneurs. Our common thread? Education.

Number of students mentored

Ever since opening our doors, we have mentored hundreds of students (ignoring masterclasses) from around the world in fields like computer science, mathematics, standardized testing, and more. Our students range from wanting simple AP guidance to months-long college admissions counselling to get into top-tier schools like ours.

Average class size

With the exception of the masterclasses, the average class size is about 2 students per course. Our courses are highly customized to the needs of its participants, with our tutors crafting custom practice problems, tailoring curriculums, and giving guidance to point anyone to success.

Learn more about our current offerings and how we can best serve your educational journey.

Standard Courses.

Standard courses are courses most may find in a high school curriculum. AP/IB courses, standardized testing, and essay editing are all exceptional examples. Whether it's to boost your GPA, get ahead of your grade, or get that standardized test score, we can help.

Specialized Courses.

For students looking for hard-to-find courses like Latin or Olympiad training, we can accomodate most requests of this nature as well. These courses will be specifically tailored by a mentor with an intricate knowledge of the subject.

Masterclasses.

For those who want to try out our programs without concerning themselves with our bidding request process. Our masterclasses are carefully-constructed courses exposing students to pre-professional fields like pre-med and machine learning or bootcamps like intensive SAT/ACT prep or a writing workshop.
