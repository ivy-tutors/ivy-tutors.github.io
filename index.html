Who We Are

A group of Ivy-League students majoring in fields from STEM to the humanities and the arts, the Ivy Tutor Academy is an organization of top-ranked students with a passion for sharing their industry experience and knowledge. Started by a group of Harvard undergraduates, our services aim to empower all students who want to learn, regardless of age or subject matter. To learn is exquisite, to master is divine.

Top-Tier Talent

Our tutors range from FAANG interns to Wall Street analysts to published authors and medical researchers. For any of our course offerings, we prioritize quality; not only will the tutor have exemplary records in that particular subject, but they will also have industry or academia experience as well to offer in your courses.

Proven Strategies

All of our tutors are carefully vetted for previous expertise and teaching style. Instructors of our Academy have taught and tutored dozens of students with exemplary results, with many previous students obtaining dramatic improvement in standardized test scores or competition results.

Continued Support and Quality

Our main priority is quality. Each course is carefully monitored by a member of our quality control team, someone wholly independent from the tutor. Any potential issues with course quality or course management will be addressed immediately.

Services

Listed are some course categories we offer. Please click on each category for more information.

Exploratory ELA (Grades 6-9)

Instead of memorizing vocabulary for a multiple choice quiz, students will be encouraged to pursue creative writing works, read current events articles, and get a leg-up in their humanities education.

Exploratory Maths (Grades 6-9)

With a flexible, personalized lesson plan, students can either get ahead in their mathematics or augment their current coursework our experienced tutors.

Exploratory Sciences (Grades 6-9)

With offerings in Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and Psychology, these courses allow students to have an appropriate primer for any science interest they may maintain.

Advanced ELA (Grades 9-12)

Assuming some knowledge of fundamental English concepts, students will tackle more rigorous ELA aid geared towards high school audiences.

Advanced Maths (Grades 9-12)

Delving into more complicated mathematics from advanced algebra to precalculus to calculus and beyond, our tutors are prepared to walk students through the complexities of mathematics.

Advanced Sciences (Grades 9-12)

With a more general focus than our AP/IB offerings, our science courses allow for exploration into more advanced topics in Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and much more.

AP Curriculum (Any)

With very few exceptions, we offer courses in all AP subjects. From AB/BC Calculus to English Language to Microeconomics, we will ensure your readiness for the AP exam and beyond.

IB Curriculum (Any)

Our tutors are heavily experienced with augmenting the IB curriculum, working alongside a student's studies to give them an advantage for the exam and beyond.

Competitions (Any)

From the AMC series to USACO to Olympiad competitions, our tutors can help students prepare for top-ranked places in these events.

College Counseling (Grades 9-12)

Our tutors are in-touch with what aspects of your application are particularly strong or weak. We provide advice for launching initiatives, strategies for applying to specific colleges, and identifying awards or scholarships.

College Essay Aid (Grades 11-12)

Students will receive top-notch aid throughout the entire process: what makes a good college essay? From brainstorming to outlining to finalizing, we do it all.

Standardized Test Preparation (Any)

From high school exams like the ISEE or SSAT or college exams like the PSAT, SAT, or ACT, our tutors will prepare you for a score you desire, whether it be for admissions, awards, or scholarships.
