Referral Program

As our mission aims towards helping as many students as possible throughout their educational journeys, we encourage students to take courses together and bring their friends into the program. With this in mind, we've created a referral program that aims to give back to our students.

If you refer a student into our program, 10% of their total payment for that specific course gets sent to you, for an amount up to $1000 USD per course. Referring multiple students allows for multiple benefits. For example, suppose Person A refers both Student A and Student B to take Course A and Course B, respectively, and Student A pays $3000 for Course A and Student B pays $12000 for Course B. As the referer, Person A receives $300 USD as a referral bonus at the end of Course A and $1000 USD as a referral bonus at the end of Course B.

When registering for courses through our form, a student has the option to list the contact information of a referer, whether it be a phone number or email address. We will subsequently reach out to the referer to obtain further details about payment.

Participation in the referral program implies acknowledgement of federal and local tax requirements. We are not liable for any contact information misspellings or delays regarding payment.
